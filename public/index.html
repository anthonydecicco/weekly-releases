Welcome to Weekly Releases.

This application will send you weekly emails on Fridays with new releases from your followed artists on Spotify.

Click the button below to authenticate with your Spotify account (you will be redirected temporarily).

By clicking "Authenticate," you agree to share your email and Spotify user information. Your information is only used to interface with the Spotify Web API and send you emails with the latest songs and albuns from your followed artists. More information about this can be found on the About page.
